Research & Innovation

Bioartificial Liver – ALEX™ Platform

Inventor & patent holder of the ALEX bioartificial liver (PCT US97/22727), featuring a novel autologous biomatrix that extends hepatocyte viability and functionality for extracorporeal support.

Hepatocyte & Gene Transplantation

Led the world’s first cell‑based gene graft to cure Crigler–Najjar syndrome via minimally invasive hepatocyte infusion, offering a pain‑free alternative to orthotopic transplant.

Robotic Stereotactic Radiosurgery

Developed fiducial‑guided Synchrony® tracking, enabling CyberKnife treatment of moving extra‑cranial tumors and achieving the first non‑invasive radiosurgery for metastatic liver tumors.

Quality‑of‑Life Metrics (PEQUOL)

Created and validated the PEQUOL questionnaire, a prognostic tool for mental and relational well‑being in transplant patients.
