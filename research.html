Core Research Themes

Bio‑artificial Liver — ALEX™ Platform

Inventor & patent holder of the ALEX bio‑artificial liver (PCT US97/22727), featuring an autologous biomatrix that extends hepatocyte viability for extracorporeal support.

Hepatocyte & Gene Transplantation

Pioneered the world’s first cell‑based gene graft curing Crigler–Najjar syndrome through minimally invasive hepatocyte infusion.

Robotic Stereotactic Radiosurgery

Developed fiducial‑guided Synchrony® tracking, enabling CyberKnife treatment of moving extra‑cranial tumours.

Quality‑of‑Life Metrics (PEQUOL)

Created and validated the PEQUOL questionnaire, a prognostic tool for mental and relational well‑being in transplant patients.
